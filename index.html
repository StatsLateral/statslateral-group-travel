Problem

Wellness coaches face major hurdles in today's evolving care landscape.

Limited income and client reach

Time-based sessions cap income and scale. Low margins make growth difficult without burnout.

Low visibility into client engagement

Coaches lack tools to monitor trust, compliance, and engagement between sessions.

Generic and time-draining tech

Delivering digital care means piecing together video hosting, website, social media, email, and client tracking tools—distracts from client time.

Solution

Wellness professionals need a smarter way to grow—without more sessions or staff.

Earn passive income and scale your reach

Turn your expertise into subscriptions, digital programs, and on-demand care—no more trading time for money.

Boost trust and retention through personalization

Deliver personalized care plans and dynamic updates based on client behavior and feedback.

Purpose-built AI platform for Wellness Coaches

Manage your entire content-based practice: subscriptions, client engagement, and digital programs—all in one place, so you can focus on clients, not tech.

How it works

Vybri helps wellness coaches grow in three distinct ways—without more sessions or staff.

1

Create once, earn passively

Takes less than 15 minutes to invite hundreds—or even thousands—of clients.

  • Create your SEO-optimized professional profile—we'll pre-build it from your website, social bio, or résumé.
  • Upload or link your program resources: videos, protocols, guides, and handouts—text, voice, and video in one place.
  • Bundle these assets into subscription programs and digital products; set your own pricing.
  • Upload your audience or sync contacts from your existing tools.
  • Send smart invitations that automatically match clients to the right program and kickstart progress.
2

Personalize plans in minutes

Takes less than 5 minutes per client.

  • Use intake notes or voice-record a quick summary; optionally pull notes from your existing tools.
  • Select—or let Vybri suggest—the recommendations, lessons, and progression methods each client needs.
  • Generate and deliver an AI-personalized plan with daily reminders, micro habits, and progress milestones.
3

Grow your brand every week

Takes less than 15 minutes per week to stay top of mind.

  • Record research topics, sessions feedback, or new ideas on your phone.
  • Let AI transform them into relevant blogs, emails, and social media posts.
  • Convert textual content to voice or video (or vice versa) instantly
  • Auto-publish across your website, YouTube shorts, newsletter, and social channels—all scheduled for you.
