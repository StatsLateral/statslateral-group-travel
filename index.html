Problem

Physiotherapists face major hurdles in today's evolving care landscape.

Limited income and client reach

Time-based consultations cap income and scale. Low margins make growth difficult without burnout.

Low visibility into client engagement

Practitioners lack tools to monitor trust, compliance, and engagement between sessions.

Generic and time-draining tech

Delivering digital care means piecing together video hosting, website, social media, email, and client tracking tools—distracts from client time.

Solution

Physiotherapists and sports wellness professionals need a smarter way to grow—without more sessions or staff.

Earn passive income and scale your reach

Turn your expertise into subscriptions, digital programs, and on-demand care—no more trading time for money.

Boost trust and retention through personalization

Deliver personalized care plans and dynamic updates based on client behavior and feedback.

Purpose-built AI platform for physiotherapists

Manage your entire content-based practice: subscriptions, client engagement, and digital programs—all in one place, so you can focus on clients, not tech.

How it works

Vybri provides three distinct ways to become AI-powered physiotherapist and sports medicine wellness coach.

1

Create once, earn passively

Takes less than 15 minutes to invite hundreds—or even thousands—of clients.

  • Create your SEO-optimized clinician profile—we'll pre-build it from your website, social bio, or résumé.
  • Upload or link your rehab resources: exercise demonstration videos, protocol PDFs, return-to-sport guides, and client handouts. All of text, voice and video content in one place.
  • Bundle these assets into subscription programs (e.g., ACL rehab series, shoulder stability course) and set your own pricing.
  • Upload inactive clients or sync from your CRM/practice tool
  • Send smart invitations that automatically match clients to the right program and reignite their recovery journey.
2

Personalize care in minutes

Takes less than 5 minutes per client.

  • Pull SOAP notes from your practice tool or voice record quick findings from your initial evaluation.
  • Select—or let Vybri suggest—the exact home-exercise videos and progression protocols each client needs.
  • Generate and deliver an AI-customized rehab plan with sets, reps, and progression cues in seconds.
3

Grow your brand every week

Takes less than 15 minutes per week to stay top of mind.

  • Record quick clinical pearls, progress milestones, or movement tips on your phone.
  • Let AI transform them into injury-prevention blogs, follow-up emails, and social carousel posts.
  • Convert content to voice or video (or vice versa)
  • Auto-publish across your website, YouTube shorts, newsletter, and social channels—all scheduled for you.
