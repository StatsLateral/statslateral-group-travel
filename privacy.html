Your continued use of the Site after changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of those changes.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Information You Provide: Contact details (e.g., name, email, phone), professional details, and any content you submit through forms or communications with us.
  • Automatic Data: Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamps), device identifiers, and usage information collected via cookies and similar technologies.
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies, pixels, and similar tools to operate and improve the Site, remember preferences, and analyze traffic.

3. How We Use Information

  • Respond to inquiries, provide support, and communicate about our services.
  • Analyze usage and performance to develop new features and offerings.
  • Protect against, identify, and prevent fraud, abuse, and security risks.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms and policies.

4. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK Visitors)

Where applicable, we process personal data based on: (a) your consent; (b) performance of a contract; (c) our legitimate interests (e.g., Site security, analytics, and product development); and/or (d) compliance with legal obligations.

5. How We Share Information

  • Service Providers: Vendors assisting with hosting, analytics, communications, and security, bound by appropriate confidentiality and data protection obligations.
  • Legal and Safety: If required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect rights, property, or safety.

We retain information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.
